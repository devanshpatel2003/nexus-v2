## Summary
Market speculation about whether new H200 chips will require export licenses. Nvidia states they are "working with Commerce Department" on classification.

## Market Impact
- NVDA 5-day reaction: -2.8%
- AMD 5-day reaction: -0.9%

## Significance
Created uncertainty about next-gen chip availability in China. Demonstrates how regulatory ambiguity itself can be a market-moving factor, even without a formal policy change.
