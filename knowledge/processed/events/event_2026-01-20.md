# New Administration Reviews Export Policy
doc_id: event_2026-01-20
date: 2026-01-20
severity: Medium
event_type: Policy Relaxation
tickers: NVDA, AMD, INTC
source: Commerce Department Statement, Reuters

## Summary
Incoming administration announces a review of AI chip export controls, signaling a potential shift from presumption of denial to case-by-case licensing.

## Market Impact
- NVDA 5-day reaction: +6.8%
- AMD 5-day reaction: +4.2%

## Significance
The market interpreted this as potential easing of restrictions. The positive reaction shows how sensitive semiconductor valuations are to regulatory direction. However, policy remains under review and no formal changes have been implemented, creating ongoing uncertainty.
